## Important Notes
*   **NodeMCU**: Ensure the board specifically mentions "ESP8266" and "Type-C". Many Type-C boards are ESP32, which is different (though usable with code adjustments).
*   **Servo**: Verify the "Control Angle" is **270°**. Standard servos are often 180°.
